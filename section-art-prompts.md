# 오늘의 크립토 섹션 이미지 프롬프트

## 공통 스타일

아래 프롬프트는 `오!디파이`, `오!탈중앙`, `오!아카이브` 섹션 카드 안에 들어갈 작은 정사각형 이미지를 만들기 위한 기본 지시문이다.

```text
Create a square editorial illustration for a Korean one-person crypto publication. Style: intentionally imperfect hand-drawn doodle mixed with light pixel-art sensibility, black ink linework on white paper, one restrained yellow accent (#ffd400), minimal composition, witty but not childish, handmade zine feeling, no glossy 3D, no gradients, no photorealism, no corporate tech aesthetic. Use thick uneven black outlines, small visual jokes, lots of white space. The image must work as a small icon inside a black-bordered square card.
```

## 오!디파이 / Oh! DeFi

```text
Create a square editorial illustration for "Oh! DeFi", a section about new finance ruled by protocols. Show a funny handmade diagram of three protocol boxes connected by arrows, with tiny coins, collateral, liquidation marks, and rule-like gears moving between them. Style: imperfect hand-drawn doodle with subtle pixel-art influence, black ink on white paper, one yellow accent (#ffd400), minimal zine-like composition, educational but playful, no realistic people, no glossy 3D, no crypto logo clutter. The image should feel like someone explaining a complicated money machine on a napkin.
```

## 오!탈중앙 / Oh! Decentralization

```text
Create a square editorial illustration for "Oh! Decentralization", a section about the promise and cracks of decentralization. Show a hand-drawn network of small nodes where some links are free and open, but one big hidden node or hand quietly pulls several strings. Style: imperfect black ink doodle with subtle pixel-art texture, white paper background, one yellow accent (#ffd400), minimal and witty, handmade zine feeling, no realistic portrait, no glossy 3D, no corporate blockchain imagery. The image should feel like a playful investigation of an ideology, not a promotional crypto poster.
```

## 오!아카이브 / Oh! Archive

영문 표기는 `Oh! Archive`가 맞다. `archaive`는 오타처럼 보인다.

```text
Create a square editorial illustration for "Oh! Archive", a section that stores past issues of a one-person crypto publication. Show a hand-drawn archive box or filing drawer stuffed with old issue papers, date labels, small bookmarks, and one yellow sticky note. Style: imperfect black ink doodle with subtle pixel-art influence, white paper background, one yellow accent (#ffd400), minimal zine-like composition, witty and warm, no glossy 3D, no photorealism, no corporate interface. The image should feel like a messy but loved drawer of previous editions.
```

## 픽셀 느낌을 더 강하게 줄 때 추가할 문장

```text
Make the linework slightly blocky, as if the doodle was redrawn on a low-resolution pixel grid, but keep it readable and editorial. Avoid retro game scenery; this is still a publication icon.
```

## 손그림 느낌을 더 강하게 줄 때 추가할 문장

```text
Emphasize uneven pen pressure, imperfect hand lettering, tiny correction marks, and rough notebook energy. Keep the drawing simple enough to read at small size.
```
